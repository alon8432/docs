1.心率

心率是指单位时间内心脏搏动的次数。一般指每分钟的心跳次数。
正常成年人安静时的心率有显著的个体差异，
平均在75次/分左右（60--100次/分之间）。

心率可因年龄、性别及其他生理情况而不同。
初生儿的心率很快，可达130次/分以上。

在成年人中，女性的心率一般比男性稍快。
同一个人，在安静或睡眠时心率减慢，运动时或情绪激动时心率加快。

在某些药物或神经体液因素的影响下，会使心率发生加快或减慢。
经常进行体力劳动和体育锻炼的人，平时心率较慢。

人体的正常脉搏（脉率）为：
男性60～100次/分钟
女性70～90次/分钟

新生儿140次/分钟

0-6岁儿童心率正常范围: 
新生儿心率120-140次/分
1岁以下110-130次/分 
2-3岁100-120次/分 (儿童约90次/分钟)
4-7岁80-100次/分，
8岁以后为人体正常心率.
正常的情况下,脉搏和心跳是同步的,心脏跳动一次,脉搏就动一次.


Heart rate is the number of heart beats per unit of time. Generally refers to the number of beats per minute.
The normal adult heart rate when there is a significant individual differences,
Average 75 times / min or so (60 - 100 times / min).

Heart rate may vary according to age, sex and other physical conditions.
Newborn heart rate quickly, up to 130 beats / min.

In adults, women generally have a slightly faster heart rate than men.
The same person slows down when quiet or asleep, and speeds up during exercise or when excited.

In some drugs or neurohumoral factors, will make the heart rate faster or slower.
Often physical labor and physical activity of people, usually slower heart rate.

The body's normal pulse (pulse rate):
Male 60 to 100 beats / min
Female 70 ~ 90 beats / min

Neonatal 140 beats / min

0-6 years old children with normal heart rate range:
Neonatal heart rate 120-140 beats / min
Under the age of 110-130 times / min
2-3 years old 100-120 beats / min (children about 90 beats / min)
4-7 years old 80-100 times / min,
After the age of 8, the body's normal heart rate.
Under normal circumstances, the pulse and heartbeat are synchronized, the heart beats once, the pulse moves once
